1-Toyota Corolla 2023: Where Elegance Meets Performance

Introduction:

Key Features:

1.

Design and Dimensions:

The Corolla sedan measures 4630mm long, 1435mm tall, and 1780mm wide, with a 2700mm wheelbase1. The hatchback version is slightly shorter, measuring 4375mm in length, with a wheelbase of 2640mm1. Both variants boast a modern, aerodynamic design that turns heads wherever you go. 2.

Engine Performance:

Equipped with a 2.0-liter engine, the Corolla delivers 169 horsepower at 6600 RPM and 151 lb-ft of torque at 4400 RPM2. Smooth acceleration and responsive handling make every drive enjoyable. 3.

Spacious Interior and Comfort:

Step inside the Corolla, and you’ll find a thoughtfully designed cabin. Comfortable seating for five passengers, with ample legroom and headspace. The sedan offers 470 liters of luggage space, while the hatchback provides between 217 liters and 333 liters1. 4.

Advanced Safety Features:

Toyota’s commitment to safety shines through with features like ABS, airbags, and stability control. The Corolla prioritizes your well-being on the road.

Infotainment and Connectivity:

Stay connected with the intuitive infotainment system. Bluetooth, USB ports, and smartphone integration keep you in control of your entertainment and navigation.

Fuel Efficiency Champion:

With impressive fuel economy, the Corolla keeps your running costs low. Ideal for daily commutes and long journeys.
