Suzuki Wagon R 2023

Introduction:

The Suzuki Wagon R 2023 is a true “tall boy” hatchback that combines practicality, efficiency, and a robust design. Whether you’re navigating city streets or embarking on a family outing, the Wagon R promises a reliable and comfortable ride.

Key Features:

Advanced K-Series Engines:

The Wagon R offers two engine options: 1.0-liter Petrol Engine: Develops 66 bhp and 89 Nm of torque. 1.2-liter Petrol Engine: Produces 88 bhp and 113 Nm of torque. Additionally, there’s a CNG version generating 52 bhp and 82 Nm of torque1.

Sturdy Design Language:

The Wagon R’s wide stance and robust design make it stand out. A true “tall boy,” it offers ample headroom and a commanding view of the road.

Auto Gear Shift (AGS):

The revolutionary AGS technology ensures effortless driving. Seamlessly switch between automatic and manual modes for a comfortable experience.

Spacious Interior:

Despite its compact exterior, the Wagon R provides ample cabin space. Comfortable seating for five passengers and practical storage options.

Safety First:

The Wagon R prioritizes safety with features like dual airbags. Its strong build and safety systems inspire confidence on every journey.
