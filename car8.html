Suzuki Cultus 2023 (Celerio): Where Efficiency Meets Practicality

Introduction:

The Suzuki Cultus 2023, also known as the Suzuki Celerio, is a compact hatchback that seamlessly combines fuel efficiency, modern features, and practical design. Whether you’re navigating city streets or embarking on a road trip, the Cultus promises a reliable and comfortable ride.

>Key Features:

Efficient Engine Options: The Cultus/Celerio is powered by a 1.0-liter dualjet, 3-cylinder engine. It produces 67 horsepower at 6,000 rpm and 90 Nm of torque at 3,500 rpm1. Available with both 5-speed manual transmission options and a front-wheel drive system.

Spacious Interior:

Despite its compact exterior, the Cultus/Celerio offers ample cabin space. Comfortable seating for five passengers, making it ideal for daily commutes.

Cargo Capacity:

Improved cargo capacity of 295 liters ensures you can carry your essentials. The 60:40 split and folding seats allow for additional space when needed2.

Infotainment and Connectivity:

Equipped with a 7-inch Sony touchscreen featuring WebLink functionality. Stay connected and entertained on the go.

Fuel Economy Champion:

With a mileage ranging from 24.97 kmpl to 34.43 km/kg, the Cultus/Celerio keeps your running costs low. Perfect for city driving and longer journeys.
